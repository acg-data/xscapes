Our Story

xScapes was founded on a simple belief: everyone deserves an outdoor space that brings joy. What started as a small design service has grown into NoVA's premier landscape architecture firm.

Our team combines artistic vision with technical expertise to create outdoor environments that are both beautiful and functional.

We've transformed hundreds of properties throughout Northern Virginia, earning a reputation for quality craftsmanship and exceptional service.
